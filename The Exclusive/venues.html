Who we are

The Exclusive is a two piece music project created by two West Chester University School of Music graduates that achieves large sonic soundscapes through the implementation of live instuments, vocals, and music technology. We would love to fill in 30-40 minutes of content as an opening act or a headlining band. For more information, please contact us at info@theexclusivemusic.com.
